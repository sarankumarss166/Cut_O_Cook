About Our Company

Cut-O-Cook is a fresh veggie delivery service started for the staff members of Kongu Engineering College and nearby residents. We offer fresh vegetables both in chopped and whole form, delivered the same day when ordered in the morning.

Our mission is to simplify your kitchen life by saving time and delivering fresh produce at your doorstep with love and care.
